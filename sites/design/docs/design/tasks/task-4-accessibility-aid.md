# Task 4 - Accessibility Aid Challenge

!!! warning "Important"
    This task must be done in groups of three or four.

## Background
**KangaLife** is a company that creates lifestyle products.  They are looking at starting a range of products to assist people with mobility and/or dexterity issues.

They are seeking prototypes for the following:

- A zipper pull extender, to assist in pulling up zips
- An aid to help turn keys
- Pen/pencil grip, to help hold a pen/pencil more easily
- Bag carrying assister to distribute shopping bag handle weight
- Keychain Pill Case, to store medication for the day
- Walking stick Clip/Hook, to hang off a table edge instead of falling over
- Card Pull Tab, attachment to make cards easier to remove from wallets

## Task
In groups of three or four, select **two** problems to solve for. Use the **Double Diamond** to assist your process. Your process must be documented as you progress. Use the [Miro Double Diamond template](https://miro.com/templates/double-diamond/) to document your process. Use templates for documenting testing and feedback.   

!!! abstract ""
    For the adventurous, try using Microsoft Forms to document feedback.

1. **Discover** – What are the problems that someone requiring the device has?  What sorts of assistance do they require?
2. **Define** – What will the device do to assist?  How will it do it?  Why is it the size that it is?
3. **Develop** – Create the 3D model in a digital environment.  Test and Document findings. Iterate if required.
4. **Deploy** – Get feedback on your design.  Iterate if required.

Whilst you have a design printing, make a start on the second.
