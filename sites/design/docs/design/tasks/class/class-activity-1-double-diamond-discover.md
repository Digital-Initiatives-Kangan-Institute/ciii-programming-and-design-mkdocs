# Class Activity 1 - Double Diamond - Discover

In groups of three or four:

1. Research a problem from a scenario below
    1. Internet
    2. Questioning
    3. Competition analysis (SWOT)
2. Come together and itemise findings
 

## Scenarios
- How might we make commuting more comfortable, productive, or enjoyable for people traveling to work or study?
- How might we help people be more productive during their day without increasing stress?
- How can we support students learn more effectively while they are on campus?
- How might we help people maintain a healthy balance between technology use and personal wellbeing?
- How can we encourage and help people to be more physically active and healthy?
- How can we get more people using electric vehicles in Victoria?
